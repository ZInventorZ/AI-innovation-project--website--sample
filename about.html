About This Project

Why we built this demo and the vision behind it.

Inspiration

The project was inspired by a desire to bring accessible form analysis to archers and coaches. By combining simple video-based feedback and session tracking, users can see measurable progress without expensive lab equipment.

Why it matters

Small, consistent adjustments compound into better performance. This tool helps make those adjustments visible and repeatable.

Vision

Enable archers worldwide to self-coach and prepare for competition with confidence and data-driven insights.
