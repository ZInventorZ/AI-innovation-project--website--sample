Use Cases & Scenarios

Who benefits from this app and how they might use it.

Beginner Archers

Short story: New archers use the AI Coach to check basic stance and anchor points after practice. Benefits: Faster progress, clear actionable tips.

  • Step-by-step walkthroughs
  • Simple form scores to track progress
Coaches

Coaches use the app to supplement in-person coaching with video-based frame analysis and session history.

  • Compare athlete sessions
  • Share concise reports with training plans
Competitive Teams

Teams track trends across sessions, identify weaknesses and tune equipment setups for competition conditions.

Clubs

Clubs can use the app as a training aid to standardize coaching across members and show measurable progress.
